**Side Quests** are little missions you can take on for **bonus Zaps** and a **badge** to keep. They
sit apart from your Journeys and your four Pillars: a Side Quest is pure extra credit, so finishing
one never shifts your Frequency Signature. It just rewards going a bit further.

## Where to find them

Open **Side Quests** under The Quest. You'll see the missions on offer, what each one is worth, and
which you've already finished.

## How they work

- Each Side Quest is a small, real-world thing to do ("Bring a friend," "Touch grass").
- Do it, then tap **Mark done**. That unlocks the **badge** and pays the **Zaps** once.
- A badge you earn this way shows up with your other badges on your profile.
- You can only finish each one once.

## Side Quests vs. the four Pillars

Your **practices** are what move your four-Pillar balance (Mind, Body, Spirit, Expression). Side
Quests don't touch that. Think of them as a bonus track: reward and a badge, not balance.
