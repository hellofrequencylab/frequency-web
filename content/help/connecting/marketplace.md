The Marketplace is a no-fee way to share things with people near you: offer something, give
it away, lend it out, or ask for what you need. No money changes hands in the app — it just
connects neighbors, and you arrange the handoff offline.

## Browsing

- Open **Marketplace** at `/market` to see what's around.
- Filter by type: **Offering**, **Free**, **To lend**, or **Looking for**.
- Tap **Near me** to sort listings by distance using your device location — the closest
  show first. Listings without a precise location fall to the bottom.

## Posting a listing

- Tap **Post a listing** and give it a title, a type, and a few details.
- Add a price or terms in plain words ("$20", "a trade", "free") — there's no checkout.
- Add photo links if you have them, and tap **Use my location** so neighbors can find it
  by distance.
- You can **edit** a listing anytime, or mark it claimed or closed when it's done.

## Making contact

- A listing links to the poster's profile, where you can reach out and arrange the details.
- Be kind and keep it local — this is mutual support, not a storefront.

## A note on privacy

Distance sorting works from your device location and shows only an approximate distance.
Frequency never publishes your exact address or a listing's precise coordinates.
