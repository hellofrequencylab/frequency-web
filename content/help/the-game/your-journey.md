A **Journey** is a seasonal set of practices — something for each part of a whole
life (Mind, Body, Spirit, and Expression) — that you move through over the season,
on your own or alongside your circle.

## Start one

Browse **Journeys** and adopt the one that fits. Adopting a Journey adds its
practices to your daily log, so it plugs straight into the practice loop you already
know — there's no separate tracker to keep.

## Follow it on your dashboard

Open **Your Journey** from your dashboard to see the whole thing at a glance:

- An ordered checklist of the Journey's practices, grouped across the four domains.
- Each step is **on track** once you've kept its rhythm this week — a daily practice
  wants most days, a weekly one just once. Your **next step** stays highlighted, with
  a quick **Log** button.
- A panel up top shows what the work earns: your season **zaps and rank**, your
  **streak**, and your **gems**. Logging a Journey's practice is the one move that
  advances the Journey *and* earns the rewards.

The current step also rides along on your home feed, so the next thing to do greets
you when you arrive.

## Doing it with your circle

If people in your circle have adopted the same Journey, you'll see **"N in your circle
doing this too."** Journeys are made to be shared — the practice is better with company.
