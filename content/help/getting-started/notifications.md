Notifications keep you in the loop without you having to check constantly: a reply to your post, a new event in your circle, a friend request, an upcoming gathering.

## Where they show up

- **In the app**: your notifications list shows recent activity.
- **Email**: for things worth leaving the app for, if you've opted in.
- **Push**: real-time alerts on your device, if you've allowed them.

## Tuning them

Head to **Settings → Notifications** to choose what you hear about and on which channels. You're in control:

- Turn categories on or off independently.
- Every email includes an unsubscribe link, and we honor it everywhere.
- Prefer calm? Keep in-app on and email light; you'll still see everything when you open the app.
