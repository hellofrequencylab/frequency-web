You are in control of who can reach you and of your account.

## Block a member

Open the member's profile and choose **Block**. When you block someone:

- neither of you can start a direct message with the other,
- you are removed as friends, and
- you stop seeing each other across the app.

You can review and undo this anytime under **Settings, Account & privacy**, where
your blocked members are listed with an **Unblock** button.

## Delete your account

Go to **Settings, Account & privacy** and find **Delete your account**. Type DELETE
to confirm, then choose **Delete account**.

This **permanently** removes your account and your content. It cannot be undone, so
make sure it is what you want. You will be signed out immediately afterward.
