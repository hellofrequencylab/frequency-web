A **journey** is a track you build from practices — a way to share how you grow with
the community. It can be as small as a single daily meditation or as deep as a full
course. It's yours; you decide how much to put into it.

## Start one

From **Journeys**, hit **New journey**. A builder window opens — give your journey a
name, pick an emoji and an accent color to give it a face, and you're in.

## Lay out the path

- **Add practices.** Search the library and tap to add any practice to your path.
  They're grouped by Pillar (Mind, Body, Spirit, Expression) so you can see the
  balance as you go.
- **Order them.** Drag the steps into the flow you want (or use the up/down arrows on
  mobile).
- **Tune each step.** Open a step to set how often it should happen and add a short
  note — "first thing, before coffee."
- **Tell the story.** Add an intro to say why this journey matters and what someone
  will get from it. A line is plenty for a simple practice; write a full guide for a
  course.

Everything **saves as you go** — there's no separate save button.

## Keep it or share it

Your journey starts **private** (just you). When you're ready:

- **Anyone with the link** — share it directly without listing it publicly.
- **Share to community** — publish it to the open library so anyone can adopt it.
  Publishing, adopting and remixing journeys are all **free**.

When someone **adopts** your journey, its practices flow into their daily loop — and
every time they log one, they earn zaps and build their streak, just like their own
practices. Others can also **remix** your journey into a version of their own.

## The one thing to remember

A journey is you, shared. Put in as much as you want — the people who adopt it are
walking a path you laid down.
