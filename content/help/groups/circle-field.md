**Field** is what your circle builds *together*. Where zaps are your personal score,
Field is the circle's — a shared, seasonal measure of the circle showing up for each
other. One number, owned by everyone.

## How it grows

- **Showing up.** Every time a member checks in at one of the circle's gatherings
  (a verified check-in — see [QR codes & checking in](/help/the-game/qr-check-ins)),
  the circle's Field grows by **10**.
- **Finishing shared challenges.** When your host has adopted a
  [season challenge](/help/the-game/season-challenges) for the circle and a member
  completes it, the Field grows by **25** — a finished challenge is a bigger lift
  than a single check-in, so it counts for more.

Your personal rewards aren't touched — you still earn your own zaps for checking in
and completing challenges. Field is what those same acts add to the *collective*.

## Where you see it

On your circle's page, a **Circle Field** card shows what you've gathered this
season. A brand-new circle simply doesn't show the card until it has gathered some
Field — there's no zero staring at you on day one.

- **Members always see it.**
- **Non-members see it only if the circle chooses to share it publicly.** By
  default, your Field is your circle's business.

## Not a competition — on purpose

Circles are never ranked against each other on Field, and there's no inter-circle
leaderboard. That's deliberate: Field exists to say *"look what we built together
this season"*, not *"we beat the Tuesday runners"*. It's collaborative all the way
down.

## Growing it

The recipe is simple: gather, and actually show up. RSVP to your circle's
[events](/help/groups/events), check in when you're there, and take on a challenge
together. Every member who walks through the door moves the number for everyone.
