Gems are the currency you **spend**, and the Vault Store is where you spend them.
Open **The Quest** and pick the **Store** tab — your Vault and the Gem Store live on
one page.

## Your Vault, up top

The top of the page is your Vault at a glance: your spendable **gem balance**, your
season **zaps** and **streak**, your locked-in **lifetime rank**, anything you've
got **equipped**, and a link to the full earning log (more on that below). What's
free vs. member is covered in [Membership & the Vault](/help/membership/the-vault).

## What's for sale

- **Profile cosmetics** — borders and flair icons that dress up your profile.
- **Custom titles** — a special title displayed on your profile.
- **Collectible badges** — exclusive badges for your collection.
- **Membership credits** — redeem gems for free membership months.

## How redeeming works

- Your balance is everything you've ever earned **minus** what you've spent — gems
  never reset, so the wallet only grows between purchases.
- Hit **Redeem** on an item you can afford. Cosmetics and titles apply to your
  profile right away, and you can own each one only once.
- Some items are **limited stock** — the card shows how many are left, and when
  they're gone, they're gone.
- Can't afford something yet? The button tells you exactly how many gems you're
  short. No surprises.

Anyone can **browse** the whole store; **spending** is the game-membership benefit.
During the beta everything is unlocked for everyone.

## "How you earned" — your ledger

From the Vault (or by tapping your gem balance), open **How you earned**: a running
log of **every gem and zap you've banked**, grouped by day, each entry labeled with
what earned it and when. Above the timeline sit your four weekly rhythms —
attendance, posting, hosting, and activity — with your current and best runs.

The rule that organizes it all: **online care earns gems, real-world showing up
earns zaps** (see [Zaps & gems](/help/the-game/zaps-and-gems)). If a number on your
profile ever surprises you, the ledger is where the answer lives.
